Vault Strategy
TAU’s AavEthena Loop Vault provides capital and fee efficient exposure to the Ethena looping strategy on Aave’s Ethereum instance. The vault runs a leveraged yield strategy on USDe and sUSDe using Aave on Ethereum. The strategy’s collateral consists of 45% USDe and 55% sUSDe to borrow USDT and leverage up collateral positions to 10.0x (89.5% LTV). The collateral composition is chosen to be eligible for the Merkl rewards distribution.
