Architecture

One vault. Every strategy.
Full control.

Fusion's modular architecture separates execution from custody. Deploy capital into Fusion Plasma Vaults, connect strategy logic through composable fuses, and let Alpha engines optimize across them, all in a plug & play fashion, fully customizable per strategy needs.

1

Fusion Vaults

ERC-4626 compliant, immutable vaults that hold your assets. Each vault is fully isolated with siloed risk and no cross-contamination. Deposit once, access every integrated market.

2

Fuse Modules

Composable, non-upgradable smart contract connectors to external protocols. Supply, borrow, swap, loop: each action is a fuse. Snap new ones in, never touch the vault.

3

Alpha Execution

Off-chain strategy engines execute onchain through governance-approved parameters. Automated rebalancing, carry trades, looping, and arbitrage, all intelligence-driven and risk-bounded.

How Fusion Vaults outperform the alternative.

Merkle proof vaults were a first step. Fusion is the leap forward: transparent, composable, and built for speed.

Feature Fusion Merkle Proof Vaults
Strategy gating Smart contract enforced, transparent onchain Proof enforced, opaque and sidechain/offchain
Trust assumptions Trust minimized Trust minimized but opaque
Onchain transparency Assets, actions, markets Execution result only
Execution Flexible, adaptable Rigid, proof must be updated for any change
Speed On the fly Governance latency
Composability Administrative, onchain configured Administrative heavy, prepare proof and verify
Technical expertise No smart contract experience required for strategy iteration Each path requires a merkle proof, explosion of paths means operational bloat
IP Protection IP can be fully protected within bespoke execution logic IP is either fully opaque or fully disclosed once proof is exposed
